LFP 105Ah Prismatic Cell

SKU: LF105

The LFP 105Ah Prismatic Cell is a high-performance lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery cell designed for energy storage applications. With its excellent safety profile, long cycle life, and high energy density, this cell is ideal for residential and commercial energy storage systems, as well as electric mobility applications.
