Our Testing Capabilities

Comprehensive testing solutions for all battery types and applications

Capacity & Energy Testing

Precise measurement of battery capacity and energy content under various discharge rates and temperatures. Our testing protocols follow industry standards including IEC 61960 and IEEE 1725 to ensure reliable, comparable results.

Power & Pulse Testing

Evaluation of battery power capability through pulse discharge testing, internal resistance measurement, and dynamic load response. Critical for applications requiring high power delivery or rapid response times.

Cycle Life Testing

Long-term cycling tests to determine battery durability and capacity retention over thousands of charge-discharge cycles. Custom cycling profiles can simulate your specific application conditions.

Thermal Performance

Comprehensive thermal characterization including heat generation during cycling, thermal conductivity, and performance across temperature extremes from -40°C to +85°C in environmental chambers.

Abuse Testing

Safety-focused testing including overcharge, over-discharge, external short circuit, crush, penetration, and thermal stability tests to validate battery safety systems and understand failure modes.

BMS Validation

Comprehensive testing of battery management system functionality including protection features, balancing algorithms, state estimation accuracy, and communication interfaces.

Advanced Data Analysis

Our testing services go beyond raw data collection. Our expert engineers apply sophisticated analysis techniques to extract meaningful insights that drive battery optimization and innovation.

Performance Modeling

Development of empirical and physics-based models to predict battery performance under various operating conditions, enabling optimization without extensive physical testing.

Failure Analysis

Detailed investigation of battery failure modes using advanced diagnostic techniques to identify root causes and develop mitigation strategies for improved reliability.

AI-Powered Analytics

Application of machine learning algorithms to battery test data for pattern recognition, anomaly detection, and predictive maintenance recommendations.
